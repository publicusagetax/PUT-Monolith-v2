# PUT Monolith FAQ

### What is the Monolith?
A compact architectural ruleset for reasoning about the PUT fiscal system.

### Is this a political document?
No. It is system-neutral and non-ideological.

### Can I use this in my own AI project?
Yes. The MIT License allows free reuse with attribution.

### Does this replace the white paper?
No. The Monolith is the foundation.  
The white paper explains the system to humans.  
The book explains the system in full narrative form.

### Why is it small?
To ensure:
- AI-ingestibility  
- Portability  
- Testability  
- Stability  
- Ease of review  

### How does it interact with AI models?
It acts as a constraint framework.  
It improves consistency, protects against contradictions, and encodes moral/ethical reasoning into AI decision-making.
